How Swipe Home Works

Most search tools give you a list and leave you to figure it out. Swipe Home watches how you engage with listings and builds a feed around your actual preferences — getting sharper every time you use it.

The Process

From Download to Dream Home

  1. 1

    Download the App

    Swipe Home is available on iOS. Download it from the App Store and create your free account in under a minute.

  2. 2

    Browse Listings

    Scroll through available homes and swipe to save, skip, or explore more detail on any listing.

  3. 3

    The App Learns What You Want

    Every interaction you have — what you save, what you skip, what you revisit — teaches Swipe Home about your real preferences. Not just bedrooms and bathrooms, but the neighborhoods, layouts, and price ranges that genuinely interest you.

  4. 4

    Your Feed Gets More Relevant Over Time

    The more you use Swipe Home, the sharper your feed becomes. Listings that match your behavior surface first. Properties that don't fit fall away. You spend less time filtering and more time finding.

  5. 5

    Connect With a Local Agent

    When you're ready to move forward, Swipe Home connects you with agents who specialize in your target market — and who can already see what you've been looking at.

Why It's Different

Built Around How You Actually Search

Not Just Filters

Filters tell you what's available. Swipe Home learns what you actually want. The platform tracks your engagement patterns and adapts your feed around your real behavior — not the boxes you checked at setup.

Built for South Florida

Swipe Home is purpose-built for Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach County. Every neighborhood, price tier, and property type in the region is indexed and searchable.

Agents Who Already Know You

When you connect with an agent through Swipe Home, they already have context on what you've engaged with. You skip the introductory conversation and go straight to the homes that matter.

Available on iOS. Free to download. Start browsing homes in Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Brickell, Coral Gables, and across Palm Beach County.
