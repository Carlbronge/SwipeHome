Built for serious buyers. Powered by behavioral intelligence.

Swipe Home is a home search app built for buyers in South Florida. Using behavioral intelligence, Swipe Home learns what you actually want from the homes you engage with and surfaces listings that match your real preferences, not just your filters.

Available in Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Brickell, Coral Gables, Wynwood, and across Palm Beach County, Swipe Home connects buyers with properties and with the agents who specialize in those neighborhoods.

For real estate professionals, Swipe Home's agent dashboard surfaces buyer intent signals before a conversation begins, giving agents a cleaner, more productive pipeline.
